Quick reminders
  • Hydrogen makes 1 bond and has 0 lone pairs.
  • Halogens (F, Br) usually make 1 bond and have 3 lone pairs.
  • Oxygen often has 2 bonds total and 2 lone pairs.
  • Nitrogen often has 3 bonds total and 1 lone pair.
